// ABOUT_ME

FROM FEELING
LIKE A FRAUD
TO SHIPPING
THINGS
PEOPLE LOVE.

I'm a product designer who thinks in systems and feels in paintings. Ten years in, I still get that buzz when a confusing flow suddenly clicks into place — and I genuinely don't think that's going away.

I've worked across fintech, SaaS, and eCommerce. The industries change, the job stays the same: figure out what people actually need, then make it undeniable. I've led design systems that went from 600 scattered code references to one source of truth overnight, shipped an award-winning pension risk tool that made finance feel almost human, and helped teams learn to trust the design process — not just the designer.

My design philosophy is embarrassingly simple: access first, ease second, aesthetics third. But I'll be honest — that third part is where I can't help myself.
